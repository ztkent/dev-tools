What is a DNS Leak?

A DNS leak occurs when your VPN fails to protect your DNS queries, revealing your browsing activity to your ISP or other third parties. This tool helps identify such leaks by checking which DNS servers are being used while your VPN is active. If servers outside your VPN's network are detected, it indicates a potential leak.
